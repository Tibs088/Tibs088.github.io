In tech I have been inspired by video games, originally going to university to study games design but shifting in the direction of software, since I found the fun in simply making computers do things. However, that games design experience isn’t wasted. Since a lot of games design is focused around making the players' experience as exciting as possible, I’m constantly conscious of the user experience. In games that would be ensuring that game mechanics were accessible and controls were intuitive, which lines up with software in making apps user friendly and readable.
